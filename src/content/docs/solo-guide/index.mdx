You've built things with Cursor, Replit, or Lovable. They worked — until they didn't. Projects stalled at 15–20 components. Context vanished between sessions. The AI kept forgetting what you'd already decided.

GSD 2 solves this by treating the AI as a junior developer who follows a structured process — not a magic autocomplete that guesses what you want. This guide walks you through adopting that process as a solo builder.
